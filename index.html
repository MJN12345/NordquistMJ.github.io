Short Bibliography

Michael is a data scientist and economist. He currently works as the data coordinator at Mad River Community Hospital. Previously, he held the position of economic research assistant, working under Professor Jesse Anttila-Hughes on a project concerning international climate regulation.

His interests lie in enhancing sustainability outcomes in global development and streamlining data reporting through automation. Michael completed his MS in International and Development Economics at the University of San Francisco (USF), and his BS in Political Science at Humboldt State University (HSU), now Cal Poly Humboldt.
